Where I'm From

I hail fom the Lake of the Ozarks, more specifically Camdenton.

Camdenton and Lake are an amazing area to grow up in. I loved it. I never realized how fortunate I was to live there until I left. It's a gorgeous area. The Lake is magnificent, albeit a little dirty. There's also Ha Ha Tonka which is just truly amazing. I have a lot of fond memories of the place and the views it hosts. Surrounding the Lake is a system of phenomenal rivers and streams, perfect for kayaking, fishing, and swimming. If you're fortunate to have a bit of land there's you'd be hosting all kinds of critters, most noteably the whitetail. They don't get very big but by golly they are tasty.
